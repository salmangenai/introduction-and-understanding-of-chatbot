Introduction to Chatbots

Chatbots are computer programs designed to simulate conversation with human users, especially over the Internet. They can provide automated responses and perform tasks based on predefined rules and algorithms.

In recent years, chatbots have gained popularity in various industries, including customer service, healthcare, and e-commerce. They offer a convenient way to interact with users and provide assistance around the clock.

Whether you're a business owner looking to enhance customer support or a developer interested in building your own chatbot, exploring the world of chatbots can be both exciting and rewarding.
